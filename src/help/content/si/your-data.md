# ඔබේ දත්ත

මෙම යෙදුම **දේශීය-ප්‍රථම** (local-first) ය: ඔබේ ලිවීම ඔබේ ස්වකීය බ්‍රවුසරයේ, ඔබේ ස්වකීය උපකරණයේ ගබඩා කෙරේ. කිසිවක් සේවාදායකයකට නොයවනු ලැබේ. යෙදුම සම්පූර්ණයෙන් offline ලෙස ක්‍රියා කරයි.

## දළ විශ්ලේෂණය

- Your work lives in **your browser** — private by default, tied to this device.
- **Export** prose and citations to take your work with you.
- **Back up** regularly; clearing site data erases your work.
- Optionally **sync** a space to a local folder.

## දේශීය-ප්‍රථම යනු කුමක් ද

ලේඛන, ඉඩකඩ, සටහන්, සහ උද්ධෘත ඔබේ බ්‍රවුසරයේ දේශීය ගබඩාවේ ගබඩා කෙරේ. ගිණුමක් නැත, ස්වයංක්‍රීය cloud sync නැත — privacy by default. Clearing your browser's site data will remove your work, so keep backups.

## අපනයනය

Export your writing to take it with you or keep a safe copy. සම්පූර්ණ ඉඩකඩ timestamped **space archive** (.zip) ලෙස export කෙරේ; උද්ධෘත [BibTeX](citations-and-bibliography) ලෙස export කෙරේ.

A space archive contains two layers: a readable tree of markdown files you can open anywhere, and a complete machine-readable copy of every record — docs, notes, attachments, annotations, citations, connections, version history, and settings.

## ආනයනය

Go to **Settings → Export / import**, choose the archive file, and it is imported as a **new space** alongside your existing ones. Importing never overwrites anything; this is also the way to move a space between devices.

## Backups, Restore සහ Sync

- Create **snapshots** from the space's backups area; each can be downloaded, restored, or deleted.
- **Restore** a snapshot to roll the whole space back to that moment. A fresh snapshot is saved first, so a restore can be undone.
- Connect a **sync folder**, set the interval, and run a sync on demand.
- The home screen shows a status chip: a warning while sync and backups are not enabled, switching to **folder sync on** once a folder is connected.

Storage is local, so **regular backups are essential**. Export before clearing browser data, switching devices, or trying something new.
