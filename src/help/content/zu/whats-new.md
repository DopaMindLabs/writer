# Okusha

Izintuthuko zamuva ku-LIpsum Writer, olusha lulokuqala. Ukuze uthole uhlwili olugcwele lwezinguquko, bheka amanothi okukhishwa kwephrojekthi ku-GitHub.

## Juni 2026

- **Isibonelo esihlukanisiwe kumafonini** — izingxenye ezimbili manje zihleli phezulu nangezansi ku-isimo esilele futhi zihlala ngempela ku-isimo solala. Bheka [Iselula](mobile).
- **Umhlolelo wedokhumenti kwiselula** — vula uhlaka, ulwazi, umlando, nezenzo kusaka ithebhu ye-**okunye**. Bheka [Umhlolelo wedokhumenti](doc-inspector).
- **Uhambo luhloniphe ukudlula** — dluza uhambo olunqindisiwe phakathi futhi uhambo luyeka ukuqala ngokwenzekayo kwamanye amanye amaskrini. Phinda noma iyiphi uhambo kusaka menyu yosizo (**?**).
- **Isimo sokuvumelanisa esihlaziyekile** — isikrini sasekhaya sibonisa isiqephu sokukhuthaza kuze ukuvumelanisa nokwenza isipele kuvulwe. Bheka [Idatha yakho](your-data).
- **Oxhumana nabo** — sithinte kusaka imenyu ye-**okunye**; ivula inkinga entsha kulandela wephrojekthi.
