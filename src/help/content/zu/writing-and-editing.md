# Ukubhala nokuhlela

Umhleli wakhiwe neindumo ende.

## Ngokufushane

- **Thayipha** — konke kugcinwa ngokwenzekayo esiphequluli sakho ngokushesha.
- **Khetha umbhalo** — igude eliwayo livela lohlobisa ngokushesha.
- **Qamba kabusha** — chofoza igama ledokhumenti ensiphweni yaphezulu.
- **Gxila** — cindezela **⌘\\** ukufihla konke ngaphandle kwamazwi akho.

## Ukugcina ngokwenzekayo

Awekho inkinobho yokugcina nalikho ingozi yokulahleka kohlolo. Ngoba isitoreji **singaselulayo**, ukubhala kwakho akuphumi kwifiso lakho ngaphandle kokukhipha — bheka ku-[Idatha yakho](your-data).

## Igude eliwayo

Khetha umbhalo uthola igude eliwayo encane lohlobisa okujwayelekile kakhulu. Uthanda izibhebhezeli? Yonke into ine-nhlelo-mfutho yayo — bheka ku-[Ukuhlobisa ne-markdown](formatting-and-markdown).

Ungavala igude eliwayo ku-[Izilungiselelo ezisheshayo](customization-and-settings#quick-settings).

## Ukuqamba kabusha idokhumenti

Chofoza igama ledokhumenti ensiphweni yaphezulu ukuqamba kabusha. Kufonini, thepha inkinobho ye-**⋮** emugweni wedokhumenti ku-isinalilelo sezibhebhezeli bese ukhetha **Qamba kabusha**. Uma ungaliqambi igama idokhumenti, isihloko siphumiswa kusaka umugqa wakho wokuqala ukuze isandla sihlale sifundeka.

## Ukuhlala ugxilile

Shintsha ku-**imodi yokugxila** (**⌘\\**) ukufihla konke ngaphandle kombhalo wakho. Bheka ku-[Izibonelo nezimodi](views-and-modes).

## Okuhlobene

- [Ukuhlobisa ne-markdown](formatting-and-markdown) — hlobisa umbhalo njengoba uthayipha.
- [Izibonelo nezimodi](views-and-modes) — bhala, gxila, funda, futhi hlukanisa.
- [BrainSpace](brainspace) — misa imiqondo ezulazulayo ecanvasi.
