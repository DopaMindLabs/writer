# Ukufinyelela

Isebenza nezibhebhezeli, abafundi besikrini, nezilungiselelo zakho zokufinyelela zohlelo. Vula **Izilungiselelo → Ukufinyelela** ukulungisa isixhumanisi. Zonke izinketho ziqala emasistimu azo, ngakho-ke akuguquki lutho kuze ukhethe wena.

## Ngokufushane

- **Isihloko & ukuphambana** — ukukhanya, ubumnyama, noma amathimu amabili anezinga eliphezulu lokuphambanisa (AAA).
- **Ukuzamazama** — fanisa uhlelo, noma phoqa ukuncitshiswa / ukugcwala.
- **Usayizi wobumbalo & isikhala** — kulisa ingqikithi yesikrini nokukhombisa imigqa.
- **Izixhumanisi & ukugxila** — izixhumanisi ezihlale zinezintambo futhi indandatho yokugxila eqinisekisiwe.
- Konke kuyinto **yokukhetha** futhi **kugcinwa ngasese**.

## Isihloko & ukuphambana

Khetha **Ukukhanya** noma **Ubumnyama**, noma enye yamathimu amabili **Anezinga eliphezulu lokuphambanisa**. Amathimu anezinga eliphezulu lokuphambanisa ahlangabezana nezinga **le-WCAG AAA** lokuphambanisa kwombhalo.

## Ukuzamazama

Khetha **Fanisa uhlelo** ukulandela izilungiselelo zohlelo lwakho lokusebenza "zokunciphisa ukuzamazama", noma phoqa ukuzamazama **Okuncishisiwe** noma **Okugcwele** ngaphandle kokulandela intando yohlelo.

## Usayizi womsebenzi nemigqa

Kulisa ingqikithi yokufunda nokubhala phezulu noma phansi, futhi engeza isikhala esitheni phakathi kwemigqa yendumo ukuze ufunde kalula.

## Izixhumanisi nokugxila

Vula **Zihlale zinezintambo izixhumanisi** ukuze izixhumanisi zingaqhindekeli kuphela embathweni. Vula **Isiqondisi sokugxila esiqinisekisiwe** sokuthola umugqa omkhulu, onezinga eliphezulu lokuphambanisa njengoba uhamba usebenzisa uhlelo ngezibhebhezeli.

## Izibhebhezeli nabafundi besikrini

- **Tab** / **Shift+Tab** hamba phakathi kwezilawuli.
- Isixhumanisi se-**skip** siqonda ngqo kunokulondolozwa okubalulekile.
- Izilawuli zinezibalo nezindima ukuze abafundi besikrini bazethule ngendlela efanele.

## Izintando zakho zigcinwa

Izinguquko zigcinwa ngasese futhi ziphindwe lapho uvula isikhelo njalo.
