# Ukuguqulwa nezilungiselelo

Lungisa isihloko, ubumbalo, nokubanzi kokufunda ukuze kukufanele. Vula isikrini **Sezilungiselelo ezijwayelekile** kusuka ku-ukuzula kwekhasi, noma guqula izinto ezibalulekile ngokushesha ku-**Izilungiselelo ezisheshayo**.

## Ngokufushane

- **Isihloko** — ukukhanya, ubumnyama, noma ukuphambana okuphezulu.
- **Ubumbalo** — ifonti yeindumo nousayizi.
- **Ukukhanya kokufunda** — ukuthi ukholomu wombhalo uhamba kude kangakanani.
- **Izinketho zomhleli** — isib. gude elisindayo.
- Izilawuli ezisetshenziswa kakhulu zihlala ku-[Izilungiselelo ezisheshayo](customization-and-settings#quick-settings).

## Izilungiselelo ezisheshayo

Isibuyekezo se-**Izilungiselelo ezisheshayo** sikunika izilawuli zansuku zonke ngaphandle kokushiya idokhumenti yakho. Sivule kusuka ku-ngosi yezikhelo. Izilawuli zihlangene ngenjongo:

- **Ukubonakala** — ukushintsha **kwesihloko** nokubanzi **kokufunda**.
- **Ukubhala** — ukushintsha **kwemodi yokugxila** nege **eliwayo**.
- **Uhambo lweziqondiso** — phinda uhambo oluncane olunqindisiwe.
- **Okunye** — okusha, ukufinyelela, nangani.
- Isixhumanisi se-**Izinkosi zosizo →** (ngendlela efanayo yokuya **ezilungiselweni ezijwayelekile →**), ngakho-ke usizo nezilungiselelo zihlala zinqinda-chofozi kunye.

## Ukuthola izilungiselelo

Isikrini sezilungiselelo ezijwayelekile sihlanganisa izinketho ku-**Izintando**, **Ukubhala**, **Idatha**, ne-**I-Akhawunti**. Iqembu ngalinye likhasi elilodwa ezihlangothini zazo ezihleli phansi phezulu. Khetha into ku-ukuzula kwengcosana futhi ikhasi liqubule kulesosigaba — futhi ukuzula kwengcosana kukhanyisa isigaba osifundayo njengoba uskrola.

## Amathimu

Khetha isihloko **sokukhanya**, **sobumnyama**, noma **sanezinga eliphezulu lokuphambanisa**. Izifanelani zanezinga eliphezulu lokuphambanisa zenzelwe ukufundeka nokufinyeleleka, futhi wonke umuntu we-isikhelo — kufaka izifaziso ne-Help Center — uyazivumelanisa ngokuzenzakalela. Bheka ku-[Ukufinyelela](accessibility) ukuze uthole isici esigcwele sokwentanda.

## Ubumbalo

Lungisa ifonti yeindumo nousayizi ukuze zifanele amehlo akho.

## Ukukhanya kokufunda

Setha ukuthi ukholomu wombhalo uhamba kude kangakanani. Isilinganiso esimfishane singenza indumo enzimile ifundeke kalula; esibanzi sigcina okwengeziwe esikrini.

## Izinketho zomhleli

Shintsha izinqubo zomhleli njenge-**gude eliwayo** ukuze zifanele indlela oyithandayo yokusebenza.

## Okuhlobene

- [Idatha yakho](your-data) — khipha, ngenisa, futhi yenza isipele somsebenzi wakho.
