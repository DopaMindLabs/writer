# Izisho nezibhaloloyi

Inani ngalinye linelobho lazo lezikhombiso lokuphatha izinkomba, ngokupheleleko **BibTeX** ngokungenisa nokukhipha.

## Ngokufushane

- **Engeza** inkomba ngesandla, noma **ngenisa** ifayela le-`.bib` / BibTeX eyabekwa.
- **Sesha**, **lungisa**, futhi **susa** amangeniso; eziphindaphindayo zidlulwa ngenkathi yokungenisa.
- **Khetha kakhulu** ukusetha uhlobo noma ususe izinto eziningi kanye kanye.
- **Khipha** iwhole lobho manje ukuya ku-`.bib`.

## Ukungenisa izinkomba

Nika noma ngenisa BibTeX futhi amangeniso engezwa ku-lobho leningi. Umfundi ufunda izinsimu ze-BibTeX ezijwayelekile kanye **nezinhlobo zamangeniso ezahlukene**, futhi ubika ukuthi zingaki ezengezwe ngokuqhekeza njengeziphindaphindayo.

## Ukulawula ulobho

- Phendlela futhi **sesha** amangeniso kujikijolo lezikhombiso; inkinobho ye-**hlanganisa** ibuyisela uhlelo olugcwele.
- Vula amangeniso wona ukuze abe **isibonelo sezimininingwane**, guqula **ukulungisa**, futhi londoloza ngamunye.
- **Khetha kakhulu** ngumugqa noma khetha konke ukusetha uhlobo noma ususe ngomculo wokuqinisekisa.

## Ukukhipha

Khipha ulobho kube BibTeX nganoma inini ukuze ulisebenzise kwamanye amaqembu noma ukugcina isipele eceleni kweindumo yakho. Bheka ku-[Idatha yakho](your-data) ukuze uthole izinketho zokukhipha ezigcwele.

## Izibhebhezeli

Ngaphakathi kujikijolo lezikhombiso:

- **Esc** — vala ijikijolo (noma khansela ukulungisa okuqhubeka).
- **Ctrl/⌘ + Enter** — londoloza amangeniso olungisayo.

## Okuhlobene

- [BrainSpace](brainspace) — xhuma imithombo nemiqondo eyixhasayo.
- [Idatha yakho](your-data) — khipha futhi yenza isipele sulobho lwakho.
