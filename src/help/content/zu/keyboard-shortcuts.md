# Izinhlelo-mfutho zezibhebhezeli

Gcina izandla zakho ezibhebhezeli. Ku-macOS sebenzisa **⌘**; ku-Windows naku-Linux sebenzisa **Ctrl**.

## Ngokufushane

- **⌘K** — vula Usizo Lwesheshayo noma ngakuphi.
- **⌘\\** — shintsha imodi yokugxila.
- **Esc** — vala isiculi noma khansela ukulungisa.
- **Ctrl/⌘ + Enter** — gcina isiqiniseko osulungisayo.

## Ezijwayelekile

- **⌘K** — vula isiculi se-**Usizo Lwesheshayo** noma ngakuphi. Sesha u-Help Center noma jika kwinhlelo-mfutho, bese ucindezela **Esc** ukuvalwa kwayo.
- **⌘\\** — shintsha [imodi yokugxila](views-and-modes#focus) vula noma vala.

## Ukuhlobisa komhleli nge-markdown

Ekunothwa ekuqaleni komugqa, lezi ziguquke njengoba ubhala:

- `# ` / `## ` / `### ` — izihloko
- `- ` / `* ` — uhlwili olunamanhlonzi
- `1. ` — uhlwili olunamanombolo
- `> ` — iqwashiso lebhulokhi

Mboza igama nge-`**` ukuthola okugqamile noma `*` ukuthola okwelengisiwe.

## Ijikijolo lezikhombiso

- **Esc** — vala ijikijolo (noma khansela ukulungisa okuqhubeka)
- **Ctrl/⌘ + Enter** — gcina amangeniso olungisayo

## Inothi ngezikhomba zesiphequluli

Ezinye izinhlanganisela zezikhomba zibekelwe isiphequluli sakho noma uhlelo lwakho lokusebenza. Lapho isikhelo sicacisa inhlelo-mfutho, ithetha ingqondo kumaskrini azo; noma ngaphi izinto zezizinda zinjengoba zijwayelekile ziyawina.
