# Iselula

Isikhelo sifanelana namaskrini amancane ukuze ukwazi ukufunda nokubhala kufonini noma ku-thebhulethi.

## Ngokufushane

- **Ibha yezithebhu ezingezansi** iyasiphezula isandla kumaskrini amancane.
- Ithebhu ye-**okunye** ivula ishalishi sinezilungiselelo, usizo, nangani.
- **Imodi yokufunda** ne-[BrainSpace](brainspace) zisetshenziswa kufonini.
- **Isibonelo esihlukanisiwe** siyatholakala kumaskrini amakhulu.

## Ukuzula kumaskrini amancane

Kumaskrini amancane isandla siyaxhoma emuva kwemenyu futhi **ukuzula kwezithebhu zangezansi** kuvela. Thepha ithebhu ukushintsha izimodi, vula **isinalilelo sezibhebhezeli** ukufika ezinaniini nezidokhumenti, futhi vula **okunye** ukuthola izilungiselelo, **Usizo**, nangani.

## Okusebenza kahle kwiselula

- Ukufunda nokubuyekeza izeduli ku-imodi **yokufunda**.
- Ukulungisa ngokushesha nokubamba imiqondo ku-[BrainSpace](brainspace).
- Ukuhlobisa okuhlelekile nge-igude eliwayo.

## Ukuqamba kabusha idokhumenti

Ku-isinalilelo sezibhebhezeli, thepha inkinobho ye-**⋮** emugweni wedokhumenti bese ukhetha **Qamba kabusha** ukupha idokhumenti igama elisha.

## Umhlolelo wedokhumenti

Vula ithebhu ye-**okunye** bese ukhetha **Umhlolelo wedokhumenti** ukuslayida [umhloleli wedokhumenti](doc-inspector) — uhlaka, ulwazi, umlando, nezenzo — ngakwesokunene.

## Okusebenza kangcono kuskrini elikhulu

- Umsebenzi wohlaka onzima phezu kwezidokhumenti eziningi.
- [Isibonelo esihlukanisiwe](views-and-modes#split) — ukuqhathanisa amadokhumenti amabili ngempela.

## Amacebo

- Isitoreji singaselulayo kusonke isiphequluli, ngakho-ke umsebenzi oqalwe kufonini wakho uhlala kufonini wakho ngaphandle kokukhipha nokuphinda ukungenisa — bheka ku-[Idatha yakho](your-data).
- Phenduka ulale ukuthola isilinganiso esikahle sokubhala.

## Okuhlobene

- [Izibonelo nezimodi](views-and-modes) — ukuthi imodi nganye ikhona ngani.
- [Idatha yakho](your-data) — ukuhambisa umsebenzi phakathi kwezifiso.
