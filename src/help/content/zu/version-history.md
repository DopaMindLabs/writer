# Umlando wezihlangothi

Yonke idokhumenti igcina umlando wayo, ukuze ukwazi ukubheka emuva ekukhuleni kohlolo futhi ubuyele kuhlangothi lwangaphambilini nganoma inini lapho udinga. Njengakho konke okunye, lo mlando ugcinwa ngasese esiphequluli sakho.

## Izinhlobo zokukhani zegciniwe

- **Isisekelo.** Isikhathi sokuqala uvula idokhumenti, isimo sayo samanje sibhalwa njengendawo yokuqala.
- **Ngokwenzekayo.** Njengoba ubhala, idokhumenti ivezwa emumva imizuzwana embalwa, kodwa kuphela lapho umbhalo ushintshe.
- **Ngesandla.** Gcina uhlangothi wena ngokwakho nganoma yiluphi isikhathi kusaka i-**Umlando** noma ithebhu ye-**Izenzo** yomhloleli wedokhumenti. Upe igama (isib. "ngaphambi kokubuyekeza") ukuze kube lula ukubona kamuva.

Izinhlobo ezenzeka ngokwenzekayo ziyasheshiswa ngokuhamba kwesikhathi ukugcina isitoreji. Izinhlobo o**zipinile** nezinhlobo ozigcinile **ngesandla** azisuswa ngokwenzekayo.

## Ukubuka nokuqhathanisa izinhlobo

Vula umhloleli wedokhumenti bese ukhetha ithebhu ye-**Umlando** ukubona uhlwili, olusha lulokuqala. Khetha **umlando wezinhlobo onke** ukuvula isibonelo sokuqhathanisa, lapho ungakhetha khona uhlangothi lwangaphambilini futhi ubone ukuthi lwehluka kanjani embhalweni wakho wamanje. Shintsha phakathi kwe-**ngamunye** diff nendawo **yengxenye eyodwa** ukuze zifanelane nendlela oyithandayo yokufunda izinguquko — umbhalo owengezwayo nombhalo osusiwe kunezibalo zamabala.

## Ukuphina uhlangothi

Phina noma iyiphi izinhlobo ukuze zigcineke ziphephile ekuhlanzweni ngokwenzekayo — kuhle kokuphawula umgomo njengohlobo oluqediwe lokuqala. Phina futhi ususe ukuphina kusaka uhlwili lwezinhlobo.

## Ukubuyisela uhlangothi lwangaphambilini

Khetha **Buyisela** kuhlangothi ukubuyisa idokhumenti kuyo. Ngaphambi kokuba idokhumenti ibhalwe kabusha, umbhalo wakho wamanje ugciniwe njengezinhlobo entsha ngokwenzekayo, ngakho-ke ukubuyisela kuhlangothi ngaso sonke isikhathi kunganezwa.
