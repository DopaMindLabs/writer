# BrainSpace

Indawo yokubamba amanothi ahlukene, imiqondo, nezingxenye ngaphambi kokuba ibe indumo. Inani ngalinye linalo elilodwa.

## Ngokufushane

- **Engeza inothi** kusuka kugude; livule ku-**ishalishi sezimininingwane** ukulilungisa.
- Lungisa **isihloko** nothi noma **umzimba** ngamunye ngqo, ngqo ecanvasi.
- Pha amanothi **uhlobo** ukuze uhlukanise imiqondo, imibuzo, nemithombo.
- Dweba **izixhumanisi** phakathi kwamanothi, futhi **hlanganisa inothi nedokhumenti**.
- Namathisela izithombe ezingaphezulu kwezimbili **ngakunye**.

## Amanothi

Engeza amanothi ngokukhululeka ecanvasi. Inothi lingabamba umqondo owejwayelekile, isitatimende, noma umsebenzi. Amanothi amasha avela ku-**isibonelo sakho samanje** — ngisho lapho uhambile usuka lapho waqala khona — ngakho-ke ikhadi oliengeziwe lihlala lapho ukhangela khona. Chofoza isihloko nothi noma umzimba **ukulilungisa ngamunye**, noma vula **ishalishi sezimininingwane** ukuze ube nesikhombise esigcwele. Chofoza isihloko nothi ukuze ube imenyu yomongo; **Escape** iyasivala. Isandla esiseceleni sibonisa **inombolo yamanothi angahlelwanga**.

## Uhlobo lwamanothi

Amanothi eza ngezinhlobo ezahlukahlukene ukuze ukwazi ukuhlukanisa umqondo kumibuzo kumithombo ngokuhlola — uhlaka oluhlelekile ngaphezu kwecanvasi ekhululekile.

## Izixhumanisi

Dweba **izixhumanisi** phakathi kwamanothi ukuze ubeke uhlelo olubonakalayo ngokuhlobana kwemiqondo.

## Izithombe

Namathisela izithombe ku-inothi kusuka kushalishi sezimininingwane (ezingaphezulu kwezimbili **ngakunye inothi**). Zigcinwa ngasese ngaphandle kokuphinda ukupakisha futhi zingasuswa nganoma inini.

### Buka isithombe esididiyelekile

Chofoza noma isithombe — isithonjana ekunothi, kushalishi sezimininingwane, noma isithombe esikhulu sekhadi lesithombe — ukulivula udidiyelekile. Lapho inothi libamba izithombe ezingaphezulu kwesinye, yenza khona kuzo ngezinsalela ezisesikrini noma izinkinobho ezintambo wekhiye Wesokunxele nesokunene. Cindezela **Escape** noma chofoza ngaphandle kwesithombe ukusivala.

### Amakhadi esithombe

Kuma-nota aqala ngesithombe, engeza ikhadi le-**isithombe** kusuka ku-gude lecanvasi. Ikhadi lesithombe libonisa isithombe esikhulu esikhundleni sombhalo womzimba, nesihloko njengenketho isigejane. Ikhadi lesithombe elisha liqala nge-**engeza isithombe** yendawo yokuwiswa — chofoza ukuze ukhethe isithombe sakho. Amakhadi esithombe abamba izithombe ezimbili futhi axhuma kwamanye amakhadi njenganoma iliphi elinye ikhadi.
