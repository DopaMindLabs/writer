# Idatha yakho

Le isikhelo **iqala ngasese**: ukubhala kwakho kugcinwa esiphequluli sakho, efisini lakho. Akukho okulayishwayo ku-iseva, futhi isikhelo sisebenza ngokuphelele ngaphandle kwintanethi.

## Ngokufushane

- Umsebenzi wakho uhlala **esiphequluli sakho** — uyimfihlo ngokuzenzakalela, uxhumene nale fiso.
- **Khipha** indumo nezisho ukuthatha umsebenzi wakho nawe.
- **Yenza isipele** njalo; ukukhulula idatha yesayithi kuyasusa umsebenzi wakho.
- Nokulinganisa **vuma** inaneli kwifolda yendawo.

## Ukuqala ngasese kumele kutsho ukuthini

- Izidokhumenti, izinani, amanothi, nezisho zihlala ku-isitoreji sendawo sesiphequluli sakho.
- Awekho i-akhawunti futhi awekho ukuvumelanisa ngokwenzekayo kwefu — imfihlo ngokuzenzakalela, kodwa idatha yakho ixhumene nalesi siphequluli kule fiso.
- Ukukhulula idatha yesayithi sesiphequluli sakho kuyasusa umsebenzi wakho, ngakho-ke gcina izipele.

## Ukukhipha

Khipha ukubhala kwakho ukuthatha nawe noma ugcine ikhophi ephephile. Inaneli lonke likhishwa njenge-**archive yenaneli** ene-timestamp (.zip); izisho zikhishwa nge-[BibTeX](citations-and-bibliography).

I-archive yenaneli iqukethe izigaba ezimbili: isihlahla esifundekayo sefayela le-markdown ongaklivula noma ngakuphi, kanye nekhophi ephelele efundwayo ngomshini yokurekhodi konke — izidokhumenti, amanothi, izamboginjo, iziphawulo, izisho, izixhumanisi, umlando wezinhlobo, nezilungiselelo — ukuze i-archive ingabuyiselwa noma ingeniswayo kamuva ngokwethembeka.

## Ukungenisa

Ngenisa inaneli kulesi siphequluli kusaka i-archive yenaneli — isib. isheduli olishutshukelayo, noma i-export yovumelanisa wefayela kusaka elinye ifiso. Iya ku-**Izilungiselelo → Khipha / ngenisa**, khetha ifayela le-archive, futhi lingeniswi njenge-**naneli elisha** eceleni nezakho ezikhona. Ukungenisa akuphindi ukukhipha okutheni; lokhu kuyindlela yokuhambisa inaneli phakathi kwezifiso.

## Izipele, ukubuyisela & ukuvumelanisa

- Dala **izisheduli** kusaka indawo yezipele yenaneli; ngalinye liqhuba umugqa ongawushutshukelayo, ubuyisele, noma ususe.
- **Buyisela** isheduli ukuguqulisa inaneli lonke kuleso sikhathi. Isimo samanje sigcinwa njengezinhlobo ezintsha kuqala, ngakho-ke ukubuyisela kuhlangothi ngaso sonke isikhathi kunganezwa. Izisheduli ezenziwa ngaphambi kokuxhasa ukubuyisela (ze-markdown kuphela) zingenqamuka ukushutshukelwa kuphela.
- Ukubuyisela kusaka **.zip** eleshutshukelayo kusebenza ngokukungenisa — i-archive iyabuya njenge-naneli elisha, futhi inaneli elikhona likhishiwe lingathintekile.
- Xhuma **ifolda yokuvumelanisa** ukufanisa inaneli ku-isitoreji sendawo, setha izikhawu, futhi yaleza ukuvumelanisa ngesidingo.
- Isikrini sasekhaya sibonisa isiqephu sesimo: isixwayiso lapho ukuvumelanisa nezipele zingavulwe, kushintsha ube **ukuvumelanisa kwefolda kuvulwe** lapho ifolda yokuvumelanisa ixhunyiwe.

Isitoreji singaselulayo, ngakho-ke **izipele ezijwayelekile zibalulekile**. Khipha ngaphambi kukukhulula idatha yesiphequluli, ukushintsha izifiso, noma ukuzama into entsha.

## Uma isikhelo singakwazi ukuqala

Uma isikhelo singakwazi ukuvula isizinda saso sendawo, sibonisa isikrini sephosela lokuqala. Kusuka khona ungakhetha **Setha kabusha idatha yendawo**, okuthi — ngemuva kokuqinisekisa okusobala — kuyasusa konke isikhelo esiligcine kulesi siphequluli futhi uqale kabusha nokuqukethwe kwesidlaliso. Lokhu kuyasusa ngokuphelele izidokhumenti, amanothi, izisho nezipele ze-in-app, ngakho-ke sicabanga njengezindlela zokugcina futhi ugcine ukukhipha endaweni ephephile.

> Le izikhelo eziguqulwa iseduli. Phatha ukukhipha kwakho njengemithombo yeqiniso futhi yenza isipele njalo.
