# Izibonelo nezimodi

Idokhumenti efanayo ingabonwa ngezindlela ezahlukene. Shintsha phakathi kwazo ngezithebhu ensiphweni yaphezulu.

## Ngokufushane

- **Bhala** — ingqikithi yokuhlelwa ngokuzenzakalela.
- **Gxila** — fihla i-chrome (**⌘\\**).
- **Funda** — isibonelo esihlanzekile esingahloleki.
- **Hlukanisa** — amadokhumenti amabili ngempela.
- **Umhloleli** — uhlaka, ulwazi, nomlando wedokhumenti yamanje.

## Bhala

Imodi ngokuzenzakalela: ingqikithi yokuhlelwa egxilisiwe enesandla nensipho yaphezulu eyatholakala lapho uzidinga.

## Gxila

Imodi yokugxila ifihla i-chrome ebiyelekile ukuze kuhlale umbhalo wakho kuphela. Yishintsha nge-**⌘\\** (Ctrl+\\ ku-Windows/Linux) — cindezela futhi ukubuyisa i-chrome.

## Funda

Isibonelo esingahloleki seindumo egcwele. Sisebenzise ukubuyekeza uhlolo njengoba umfundi wakho azosibona.

## Hlukanisa

Isibonelo esihlukanisiwe sibonisa amadokhumenti amabili ngempela — esihle ukuphendula amanothi njengoba ubhala, noma ukuqhathanisa izeduli ezimbili. Siyatholakala kumaskrini amakhulu.

## Umhloleli

Umhloleli uveza **uhlaka** lwedokhumenti, ulwazi, nomlando. Shintsha usayizi wawo kusukela okufihlekile, kuze kube izikhumba, kuze kube sande. Uhlaka lwakhiwa ngezihloko zakho, ngakho-ke izihloko ezinhle zenza ukuzula kube ngokushesha.

## Ukukhanya kokufunda
