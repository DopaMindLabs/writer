# Ukuhlobisa ne-markdown

Hlobisa umbhalo ngezindlela ezintathu: igude eliwayo, izinhlelo-mfutho zezibhebhezeli, noma izinhlelo-mfutho ze-markdown ezithayipiwe njengoba ubhala.

## Ngokufushane

- Thayipha `## ` ukuthola isihloko, `- ` ukuthola inhlonzi, `1. ` ukuthola uhlwili olunamanombolo.
- Mboza igama nge-`**bold**` noma `*italic*`.
- Khetha umbhalo ukusebenzisa igude eliwayo esikhundleni salokho.

## Izinhlelo-mfutho ze-Markdown

Thayipha lezi ekuqaleni komugqa (noma ngaselwini) futhi ziguquke ngokwenzekayo:

- `# ` → isihloko 1, `## ` → isihloko 2, `### ` → isihloko 3
- `- ` noma `* ` → uhlwili olunamanhlonzi
- `1. ` → uhlwili olunamanombolo
- `> ` → iqwashiso lebhulokhi
- `**bold**` → **bold**, `*italic*` → *italic*

## Ukuhlobisa okungaphakathi

Khetha umbhalo futhi usebenzise igude eliwayo, noma usebenzise izibhebhezeli:

- **Okugqamile** — inhlelo-mfutho yokugqamile
- *Okwelengisiwe* — inhlelo-mfutho yokwelengisa

## Izihloko nohlelo

Izihloko zichaza **uhlaka** lwedokhumenti oluvezwa kumhloleli. Zisebenzise ukugcina izindaba ezinde ziyingeleleka.

## Olulwimi nezingcaphuno

Olulwimi nezingcaphuno zebhulokhi kugcina ukuhlobisa kwazo lapho ucindezela Enter, ngakho-ke ungaqhubeka uthayipha ngaphandle kokufinyelela inqangathi. Cindezela Enter ku-into yohlwili elingenalutho ukuphuma kuhlwili.
