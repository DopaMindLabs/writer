# What's new

The latest improvements to LIpsum Writer, newest first. For the full change
log, see the project's release notes on GitHub.

## June 2026

- **Split view on phones** — the two panes now stack top and bottom in
  portrait and sit side by side in landscape. See [Mobile](mobile).
- **Doc inspector on mobile** — open the outline, info, history, and actions
  from the **more** tab. See [The document inspector](doc-inspector).
- **Tours respect a skip** — dismiss a guided tour part-way and tours stop
  auto-starting on other screens. Replay any tour from the help (**?**) menu.
- **Sync status at a glance** — the home screen shows a warning chip until
  sync and backups are enabled. See [Your data](your-data).
- **Contact** — reach us from the **more** menu; it opens a new issue on the
  project's tracker.
