# All features

Everything the app can do, in one scannable list. Each item links to its guide
so you can jump straight to the detail.

## Writing & editing

- **Autosave** — every keystroke saved locally, no save button. [Learn more](writing-and-editing#autosave)
- **Floating toolbar** — quick formatting on text selection. [Learn more](writing-and-editing#the-floating-toolbar)
- **Rename documents** — click the title in the top bar. [Learn more](writing-and-editing#renaming-a-document)
- **Persistence** — your draft survives a hard reload. [Learn more](your-data#what-local-first-means)

## Formatting

- **Markdown shorthand** — headings, lists, quotes, bold/italic as you type. [Learn more](formatting-and-markdown#markdown-shortcuts)
- **Headings build the outline** — structure long pieces. [Learn more](formatting-and-markdown#headings-and-structure)

## Organising

- **Spaces** — self-contained projects. [Learn more](organizing-your-work#spaces)
- **Sections & documents** — add, rename, and reorder from the sidebar. [Learn more](organizing-your-work#sections-and-documents)
- **Templates** — start from a structure, not a blank page. [Learn more](organizing-your-work#templates)

## Views & modes

- **Write** — the default editing surface. [Learn more](views-and-modes#write)
- **Focus mode** — hide the chrome with ⌘\\. [Learn more](views-and-modes#focus)
- **Read** — a clean, non-editable view. [Learn more](views-and-modes#read)
- **Split** — two documents side by side. [Learn more](views-and-modes#split)
- **Inspector** — outline, info, and history. [Learn more](views-and-modes#the-inspector)

## Citations

- **Import BibTeX** — paste or upload `.bib`, with duplicate skipping. [Learn more](citations-and-bibliography#importing-references)
- **Manage entries** — search, edit inline, bulk-select, delete. [Learn more](citations-and-bibliography#managing-the-library)
- **Export to `.bib`** — take your library anywhere. [Learn more](citations-and-bibliography#exporting)

## BrainSpace

- **Notes** — capture and edit ideas on a canvas. [Learn more](brainspace#notes)
- **Note kinds** — tell ideas, questions, and sources apart. [Learn more](brainspace#note-kinds)
- **Connections** — map how notes relate. [Learn more](brainspace#connections)
- **Images** — up to two per note. [Learn more](brainspace#images)
- **Full-size viewer** — click a picture to open it full size. [Learn more](brainspace#view-a-picture-full-size)
- **Image cards** — picture-first notes from the toolbar. [Learn more](brainspace#image-cards)

## Annotations

- **Highlights** — a theme-aware colour palette. [Learn more](annotations-and-highlights#highlighting)
- **Annotations** — notes attached to a passage. [Learn more](annotations-and-highlights#annotations)

## Customisation
