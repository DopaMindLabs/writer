# Organising your work

A simple hierarchy keeps projects tidy: **spaces** contain **sections**, and
sections contain **documents**.

## At a glance

- **Space** — one self-contained project (a book, a course, a topic).
- **Section** — a group of related documents inside a space.
- **Document** — the unit you actually write in.
- Start a new space from a **template** to skip the blank page.

## Spaces

A space is a self-contained project with its own sidebar, settings, and
[BrainSpace](brainspace) canvas. Switch spaces from the sidebar or the **Home**
screen.

## Sections and documents

Within a space, group related documents into sections. **Add**, **rename**, and
reorder documents from the sidebar. Press **Escape** to cancel an add or rename
without saving.

## Templates

When you create something new, start from a **template** instead of a blank
page. Templates pre-fill a structure — sections and starter documents — so you
begin with momentum.

## Renaming and tidying

- Click any name in the sidebar to rename it; **Enter** commits, **Escape** reverts.
- Keep documents short and focused; lean on sections for structure.
- Use clear titles so search and the sidebar stay scannable.
