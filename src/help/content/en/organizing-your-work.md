# Organizing your work

Your writing is organized in a simple hierarchy: **spaces** contain
**sections**, and sections contain **documents**.

## Spaces

A space is a self-contained project — a book, a course, a research topic. Each
space has its own sidebar, settings, and BrainSpace canvas. Switch spaces from
the sidebar or the Home screen.

## Sections and documents

Within a space, group related documents into sections. Add, rename, and reorder
documents from the sidebar. A document is the unit you actually write in.

## Templates

When you create something new, you can start from a **template** instead of a
blank page. Templates pre-fill a structure — sections and starter documents — so
you can begin with momentum rather than a blank canvas.

## Renaming and tidying

- Click any name in the sidebar to rename it.
- Keep documents short and focused; lean on sections for structure.
- Use clear titles so search and the sidebar stay scannable.
