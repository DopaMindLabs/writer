# Customization & settings

Make the app feel like yours. Settings are found from the **Settings** link in
the page navigation.

## Themes

Choose between light, dark, and high-contrast themes. High-contrast variants are
designed for readability and accessibility, and every part of the app —
including highlights and the Help Center — adapts automatically.

## Typography

Tune the prose font and size to suit your eyes. Long writing sessions are kinder
when the type is set the way you like it.

## Reading width

Set how wide the text column runs. A narrower measure can make dense prose
easier to read; a wider one fits more on screen.

## Editor options

Toggle editor behaviors such as the floating toolbar to match how you prefer to
work.
