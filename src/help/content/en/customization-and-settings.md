# Customisation & settings

Make the app feel like yours. Open the full **Settings** screen from the page
navigation, or change the essentials fast from **Quick settings**.

## At a glance

- **Theme** — light, dark, or high contrast.
- **Typography** — prose font and size.
- **Reading width** — how wide the text column runs.
- **Editor options** — e.g. the floating toolbar.
- Most-used controls live in [Quick settings](customization-and-settings#quick-settings).

## Quick settings

The **Quick settings** popover gives you the everyday controls without leaving
your document. Open it from the app rail. It includes:

- **Theme** switch and **reading width**.
- **Focus mode** and **floating toolbar** toggles.
- **Replay a guided tour**.
- A **help center →** link (the same way it links to **full settings →**), so
  help and settings are always one click away.

## Finding a setting

The full Settings screen groups options into **Preferences**, **Writing**,
**Data**, and **Account**. Each group is a single page with its sections stacked
top to bottom. Pick an item in the side navigation and the page scrolls straight
to that section — and the navigation highlights the section you're reading as
you scroll.

## Themes

Choose **light**, **dark**, or a **high-contrast** theme. High-contrast variants
are designed for readability and accessibility, and every part of the app —
including highlights and the Help Center — adapts automatically. See
[Accessibility](accessibility) for the full preference layer.

## Typography

Tune the prose font and size to suit your eyes. Long writing sessions are kinder
when the type is set the way you like it.

## Reading width

Set how wide the text column runs. A narrower measure can make dense prose
easier to read; a wider one fits more on screen.

## Editor options

Toggle editor behaviours such as the **floating toolbar** to match how you
prefer to work.
