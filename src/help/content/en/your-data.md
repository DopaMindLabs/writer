# Your data

This app is **local-first**: your writing is stored in your own browser, on your
own device. Nothing is uploaded to a server, and the app works fully offline.

## What local-first means

- Your documents, spaces, notes, and citations live in your browser's local
  storage.
- There is no account and no sync — which means privacy by default, but also
  that your data is tied to this browser on this device.
- Clearing your browser's site data will remove your work, so keep backups.

## Exporting

Export your writing to take it with you or to keep a safe copy. Citations can be
exported as [BibTeX](citations-and-bibliography), and your prose can be exported
for use elsewhere.

## Importing

Bring work in by importing a previous export. Importing merges the content into
your current library.

## Backups

Because storage is local, **regular backups are your safety net**. Export
periodically — especially before clearing browser data, switching devices, or
trying something experimental.

> This is an evolving, experimental app. Treat your exports as the source of
> truth and back up often.
