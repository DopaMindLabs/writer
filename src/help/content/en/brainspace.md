# BrainSpace

BrainSpace is a canvas for thinking. It is the place to capture loose notes,
ideas, and fragments before they become prose — every space has one.

## Notes

Add notes freely onto the canvas. A note can hold a quick thought, a quotation,
or a task. Open a note to see and edit its full detail.

## Note kinds

Notes come in different **kinds** so you can tell an idea from a question from a
source at a glance. Use kinds to bring a light structure to an otherwise free
canvas.

## Connections

Draw **connections** between notes to map how ideas relate. The web of
connections often reveals the shape of an argument before you have written a
word of it.

## Working alongside your draft

Use [Split view](views-and-modes) to keep BrainSpace beside your document, so
you can pull captured ideas into the draft as you write.
