# Tuhituhi & ētita

Ko te ētita i hangaia mō ngā kuputuhi roa.

## I roto i te tirohanga

- **Pātuhi** — ka tiaki aunoa ki tōu pūtirotiro tōtika.
- **Kōwhiria kuputuhi** — ka puta tētahi paepae rauemi rere mō te whakarite tere.
- **Whakaingoa anō** — pāwhiria ingoa tuhinga i roto i te paepae o runga.
- **Arotahi** — pātuhi **⌘\\** hei huna i ngā mea katoa engari ōu kupu.

## Tiaki aunoa

Kāore he pātene tiaki, kāore he tūraru o te ngaro o tētahi rorohiko tuhituhi. I te mea he
**ōkawa-ōkawa** te rokiroki, kāore e rere ana tōu tuhituhi i tōu pūrere tae noa ki tōu
kaweatu — tirohia [Ō Raraunga](your-data).

## Te paepae rauemi rere

Kōwhiria tēhea kuputuhi hei whakaatu i tētahi paepae rauemi rere iti mō ngā whakarite
e whakamahia ana rawa atu. E hiahia ana ki te papapātuhi? Ko ia mahi he tohutohu anō — tirohia
[Whakarite & mākoha](formatting-and-markdown).

Ka taea e koe te huri i te paepae rauemi rere ki raro i
[Tautuhinga Tere](customization-and-settings#quick-settings).

## Te whakaingoa anō i tētahi tuhinga

Pāwhiria ingoa tuhinga i roto i te paepae o runga hei whakaingoa anō. I runga i tētahi waea, pāwhiria
te pātene **⋮** i runga i tētahi raina tuhinga i roto i te kete whakatere ā, kōwhiria **Whakaingoa anō**. Mēnā
kāore e whakaingoatia ana tētahi tuhinga, ka tautohuia tētahi taitara mai i tōu raina tuatahi kia
noho āta tirohia ai te pātaka.

## Te noho arotahi

Hurihia ki **te aratahi arotahi** (**⌘\\**) hei huna i ngā mea katoa engari ōu kuputuhi. Tirohia
[Ngā tirohanga & aratahi](views-and-modes).

## Pānga

- [BrainSpace](brainspace) — tūparia ngā whakaaro kē ki runga i tētahi ataahua.
