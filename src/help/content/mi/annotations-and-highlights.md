# Tuhipoka me ngā tōrangitia

Tohua tō kuputuhi hei tohu i ngā wāhanga, hei waiho i ngā meōhake ki a koe anō, ā, hei
aroturuki i ngā mea e hiahiatia ana ki te mahi.

## I roto i te tirohanga

- **Kōwhiria kuputuhi** ā whakaarohia tētahi **tōrangitia** mai i te tāhū tae.
- Whakamahia ngā tae hei waehere — hei tauira, kōwhai "huri anō", māwhero "raina kaha".
- Ka urutau ngā tōrangitia ki tō kaupeka, ka noho mōhiotia ki ngā wāhi katoa.

## Tōrangitia

Kōwhiria kuputuhi ā tāruatia tētahi tōrangitia. Ka whakamahi ngā tōrangitia i tētahi **tāhū** iti o ngā tae
kia taea ai e koe te hanga i tōu ake tikanga.

## Te kōwhiri i tētahi tāhū

He rite tonu te tāhū tōrangitia mā tō tuhituhi katoa, ā, ka **whai i te kaupeka o nāianei**, nō reira ka noho
mōhiotia ngā tōrangitia i roto i ngā āhua mārama, pouri, me te tairoa-teitei hoki. Tāera kaupeka me te āhua i
[Whakarite & tautuhinga](customization-and-settings).

## Tuhipoka

Tāpirihia tētahi meōhake ki tētahi wāhanga ina kore e rahi rawa ana tētahi tōrangitia. Ka pupuri ngā tuhipoka
i ō whakaaro ētita tata ki ngā kuputuhi e hāngai ana, me te kore e whakanguru i ngā kuputuhi ake.

## Ngā āpōpō

- Whakatauhia te tikanga o ia tae i mua i tō tīmatanga, ā, pūmautia.
- Whakamahia ngā tōrangitia hei rārangi arotake — mukua i a koe e whakatau ana i tēnā, i tēnā.
- Huri tētahi meōhake ki tētahi whakaaro ake i roto i [BrainSpace](brainspace).

## Pānga

- [BrainSpace](brainspace) — whakawhanaketia tētahi whakaaro kua tohua.
- [Whakarite & tautuhinga](customization-and-settings) — ngā kaupeka me te āhua.
