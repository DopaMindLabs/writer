# Waea pūkoro

Ka urutau te taupānga ki ngā mata iti kia āhei ai koe ki te pānui me te tuhituhi i runga i tētahi waea
rānei papaaho.

## I roto i te tirohanga

- Ko tētahi **paepae tānga raro** ka huri i te pātaka i runga i ngā mata iti.
- Ka tuwhera te tānga **anō** i tētahi kete me ngā tautuhinga, āwhina, me ngā mōhiohio.
- Ko ngā aratahi **Pānui** me [BrainSpace](brainspace) ka āhei i runga i tētahi waea.
- Ko **Tirohanga wehe** ka āhei i runga i ngā mata nui ake.

## Whakatere i runga i ngā mata iti

I runga i ngā mata iti ka tōhea te pātaka ki muri i tētahi tahua, ā, ka puta tētahi whakatere
**tānga-raro**. Pāwhiria tētahi tānga hei huri aratahi, tuwhera i te **kete whakatere** hei
tae ki ngā wāhi me ngā tuhinga, ā, tuwhera **anō** mō ngā tautuhinga, **Āwhina**, me ngā
mōhiohio.

## Ngā mea e pai ana i runga i ngā waea pūkoro

- Te pānui me te arotake i ngā rorohiko tuhituhi i roto i te aratahi **pānui**.
- Ngā ētita tere me te hopu whakaaro i roto i [BrainSpace](brainspace).
- Whakarite māmā mā te paepae rauemi rere.

## Te whakaingoa anō i tētahi tuhinga

I roto i te kete whakatere, pāwhiria te pātene **⋮** i runga i tētahi raina tuhinga ā, kōwhiria
**Whakaingoa anō** hei homai i tētahi ingoa hou ki te tuhinga.

## Te kaitirotiro tuhinga

Tuwhera te tānga **anō** ā, kōwhiria **Kaitirotiro tuhinga** hei heke mai
[i te kaitirotiro tuhinga](doc-inspector) — āhua, mōhiohio, hītori, me ngā mahi —
mai i te taha matau.

## Ngā mea pai ake i runga i tētahi mata nui ake

- Mahi hanganga taumaha mā ngā tuhinga maha.
- [Tirohanga wehe](views-and-modes#split) — te whakatairite i ngā tuhinga e rua taha-ki-taha.

## Ngā āpōpō

- Ko te rokiroki ōkawa-ōkawa ki ia pūtirotiro, nō reira ko te mahi i tīmata ai i runga i tōu
  waea ka noho i tōu waea tae noa ki tōu kaweatu me kawemai — tirohia [Ō Raraunga](your-data).
- Hurihia ki te āhua pāpaho hei āhua tuhituhi pai ake.

## Pānga

- [Ngā tirohanga & aratahi](views-and-modes) — te tikanga o ia aratahi.
- [Ō Raraunga](your-data) — te neke i ngā mahi i waenga i ngā pūrere.
