# He aha ngā mea hou

Ko ngā whakapaipai hou rawa atu ki LIpsum Writer, hou-tuatahi. Mō te rārangi
hurihanga katoa, tirohia ngā tuhipoka tukuhanga o te kaupeka i runga i GitHub.

## Hune 2026

- **Tirohanga wehe i runga i ngā waea** — ko ngā papa e rua ka tāritihia ki runga me raro i roto i
  te āhua tū me te noho taha-ki-taha i roto i te āhua takoto. Tirohia [Waea pūkoro](mobile).
- **Kaitirotiro tuhinga i runga i ngā waea pūkoro** — tuwhera te āhua, mōhiohio, hītori, me ngā mahi
  mai i te tānga **anō**. Tirohia [Te kaitirotiro tuhinga](doc-inspector).
- **Ka whai haerenga i tētahi tāmatea** — whakakitea tētahi haerenga ārahi mā-waenga, ā, ka mutu ngā
  haerenga ki te tīmata aunoa i ērā atu mata. Tākaro anō tētahi haerenga mai i te tahua āwhina (**?**).
- **Āhua ohaoha i te tirohanga** — ka whakaatu te mata kāinga i tētahi maramara whakatūpato tae noa ki
  te whakahoahoa me ngā tiaki tūāhuatanga e whakaorangia ana. Tirohia [Ō Raraunga](your-data).
- **Whakapā** — tae ki a mātou mai i te tahua **anō**; ka tuwhera i tētahi take hou i runga i te
  aroturuki o te kaupeka.
