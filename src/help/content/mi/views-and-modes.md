# Ngā tirohanga & aratahi

Ka taea e te tuhinga kotahi te tiro mā ngā huarahi rereke. Huriā i waenga rātou mā ngā
tānga i roto i te paepae o runga.

## I roto i te tirohanga

- **Tuhituhi** — te mata ētita taunoa.
- **Arotahi** — hunaia te kāwai (**⌘\\**).
- **Pānui** — tētahi tirohanga pānui māmā, kore-ētita.
- **Wehe** — ngā tuhinga e rua taha-ki-taha.
- **Kaitirotiro** — āhua, mōhiohio, me te hītori mō te tuhinga o nāianei.

## Tuhituhi

Ko te aratahi taunoa: tētahi mata ētita arotahi me te pātaka me te paepae o runga
ka āhei i tōu hiahia.

## Arotahi

Ka huna e te aratahi arotahi te kāwai huri noa kia noho anake tōu kuputuhi. Hurihia
me **⌘\\** (Ctrl+\\ i runga i Windows/Linux) — pātuhi anō hei hoki mai i te kāwai.

## Pānui

Tētahi tirohanga pānui kore-ētita o ngā kuputuhi kua oti. Whakamahia hei arotake i tētahi
rorohiko tuhituhi pērā i te kite a tōu kaipānui.

## Wehe

Ka whakaatu te tirohanga wehe i ngā tuhinga e rua taha-ki-taha — he pai mō te
tohungatanga meōhake i a koe e tuhituhi ana, rānei te whakatairite i ngā rorohiko tuhituhi
e rua. Ka āhei i runga i ngā mata nui ake.

## Te kaitirotiro

Ka whakaatu te kaitirotiro i te **āhua** tuhinga, mōhiohio, me te hītori. Huripohia tōna
rahi mai i huna, ki ngā tohu, ki te whakawhānui. Ko te āhua i hanga mai i ōu tūāhua, nō reira
ko ngā tūāhua pai ka tere te whakatere.

## Whānuitanga pānui

E hiahia ana ki tētahi āhua kitea-iti rānei nui? Tāeratia te **whānuitanga pānui** i roto i
[Tautuhinga Tere](customization-and-settings#quick-settings) hei hāngai ki ōu karu
me tōu mata.
