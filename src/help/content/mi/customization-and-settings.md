# Whakarite & tautuhinga

Tāeratia te kaupeka, te momotuhi, me te whānuitanga pānui hei hāngai ki a koe. Tuwhera te
mata **Tautuhinga ā-Ao** mai i te whakatere whārangi, rānei hurihia ngā mea nui tere
mai i **Tautuhinga Tere**.

## I roto i te tirohanga

- **Kaupeka** — mārama, pouri, rānei tairoa-teitei.
- **Momotuhi** — momotuhi kuputuhi me te rahi.
- **Whānuitanga pānui** — te whānui o te koro kuputuhi.
- **Ngā kōwhiringa ētita** — hei tauira, te paepae rauemi rere.
- Ko ngā mana e whakamahia ana ana i roto i [Tautuhinga Tere](customization-and-settings#quick-settings).

## Tautuhinga Tere

Ka homai e te **Tautuhinga Tere** ngā mana o ia rā me te kore e wehe i tōu tuhinga. Tuwhera
mai i te reli taupānga. Ka rōpūhia ngā mana mā te kaupeka:

- **Āhua** — te huringa **kaupeka** me te **whānuitanga pānui**.
- **Tuhituhi** — ngā huringa **aratahi arotahi** me te **paepae rauemi rere**.
- **Haerenga ārahi** — tākaro anō tētahi haerenga ārahi.
- **Atu** — ngā mea hou, āheitanga, me ngā mōhiohio.
- Tētahi hononga **tari āwhina →** (pērā tonu te hono ki **tautuhinga ā-ao →**), nō reira
  ko te āwhina me ngā tautuhinga ka noho i te pāwhiri kotahi.

## Te kimi i tētahi tautuhinga

Ka rōpū i ngā kōwhiringa e te mata Tautuhinga ā-Ao ki **Ngā Hiahia**, **Tuhituhi**,
**Raraunga**, me te **Pūkete**. Ko ia rōpū he whārangi kotahi me ōna wāhanga kua tāritihia
ki runga ki raro. Kōwhiria tētahi āporo i roto i te whakatere taha, ā, ka āputa tōtika te
whārangi ki taua wāhanga — ā, ka tohu te whakatere i te wāhanga e pānuitia ana e koe
i a koe e āputa ana.

## Ngā kaupeka

Kōwhiria **mārama**, **pouri**, rānei tētahi kaupeka **tairoa-teitei**. Ko ngā āhuatanga
tairoa-teitei i hangaia mō te māmātanga me te āheitanga, ā, ko ia wāhanga o te taupānga —
tae atu ki ngā tōrangitia me te Tari Āwhina — ka urutau autonoa. Tirohia
[Āheitanga](accessibility) mō te papa hiahia katoa.

## Momotuhi

Tāera te momotuhi kuputuhi me te rahi hei hāngai ki ōu karu.

## Whānuitanga pānui

Tautuhia te whānui o te koro kuputuhi. Ka āhei tētahi āhua kitea-iti ki te āwhina i ngā kuputuhi
makekū ki te pānui; ka hāngai tētahi āhua nui ki te whakaatu mea maha i runga i te mata.

## Ngā kōwhiringa ētita

Hurihia ngā whanonga ētita pērā i te **paepae rauemi rere** hei hāngai ki tōu tikanga mahi.
