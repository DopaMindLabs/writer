# Te kaitirotiro tuhinga

Ko te kaitirotiro tuhinga ko te papa i taha o te ētita. Ko tōna tānga **Āhua** e mahere ana i te
hanganga o te tuhinga, ā, ko tōna tānga **Mōhiohio** e whakaatu ana i ngā taipitopito ora mō te tuhinga
e tuhituhi ana koe, ā, ka āhei koe ki te tautuhia ētahi tepe me tētahi āhua. Ka kōwhiri koe ko ēhea
o ēnei mara ka puta — ā-ao me ia wāhi — kia whakaatu noa te kaitirotiro i ngā mea e hiahia ana koe.

## Te āhua

Ka rārangi te tānga **Āhua** i ngā tūāhua o te tuhinga i roto i te raupapa, i tōia ki te taumata, kia
āhei ai koe ki te kite i te āhua o tētahi mahi roa i te tirohanga. Ka hou ia wā e tū nei koe i muri i tōu
tū ki te pātuhi.

Ko te āhua i hanga mai i ōu tūāhua — pātuhi `# `, `## ` rānei `### ` i te tīmatanga o
tētahi raina (rānei whakamahia te paepae rauemi rere) hei tāpiri. Ka whakaatu tētahi tuhinga kore
tūāhua i tētahi āhua kūare; tirohia [Whakarite & mākoha](formatting-and-markdown#headings-and-structure) mō
ngā āhua o ngā tūāhua.

I runga i tētahi waea, tuwhera te kaitirotiro mai i te tānga **anō**: kōwhiria **Kaitirotiro tuhinga** mai i
te tahua, ā, ka heke mai i te taha matau.

## Ngā rahinga kupu me ngā ā-tohu

Ka whakaatu tonu te tānga Mōhiohio i ngā rahinga **kupu** me **ā-tohu** ora mō te tuhinga.

## Te tautuhia tētahi tepe kupu rānei ā-tohu

Mēnā ka whakaaturia te mara **Tepe kupu** rānei **Tepe ā-tohu**, pātuhi he tau hei tautuhia tētahi
whāinga. Ka pānui ai te rahinga `o nāianei / tepe`, ā, i te waeatia ake koe i runga i te tepe ka huri
whero te rahinga, ā, ka tōrangitia ngā kuputuhi roto-tepe i roto i te ētita. Kāore he mea e mukua ana
rānei e tapahia ana — ko te tōrangitia he tohu ā-kanohi anake, nō reira ka taea tonu e koe te tuhituhi
me te tapihi ā muri.

Waihotia he tepe kūare (rānei tautuhia ki `0`) mō tētahi tepe kore.

Mēnā e hiahia ana koe ki te pupuri i te tepe me te kaitatau engari kāore i ngā tōrangitia ētita, huria
**Tohu kuputuhi roto-tepe** ki te whakatau tukuraro i ngā tautuhinga (tirohia i raro).

## Āhua me te raka

Ko te **Āhua** picker e neke ana i tētahi tuhinga mā ōna taumata: _Rorohiko_, _E haere ana_, _I roto i te
arotake_, _Kua oti_ me _Kua whakaputaina_. Ka raka te tautuhia i te āhua ki **Kua oti** rānei **Kua whakaputaina**
i te tuhinga kia kore ai e hurihia mā te hapa — ka puta tētahi panui ki runga i te ētita me tētahi pātene
**Wetewetea hei ētita**. Hei wetewete, whakamahia taua pātene rānei huria te āhua ki tētahi taumata o mua.
Ko te raka e tiaki ana anake i te tinana o te tuhinga; kāore he mea e mukua ana.

## Rā ea

Mēnā ka whakaaturia te mara **Rā ea**, kōwhiria tētahi rā hei tuhia tētahi arotahi. Ka whakaaturia tētahi
rā kua roa i roto i te whero.

## Te kōwhiri i ngā mara ka puta

He kōwhiri noa ngā mara katoa o te kaitirotiro. Hei kōwhiri ko ēhea ka puta:

- **Ā-ao:** tuwhera **Tautuhinga ā-ao → Kaitirotiro tuhinga** ā, hurihia ia mara ki runga rānei iho. Ka
  āhei hoki koe ki te kōwhiri ko ēhea taumata āhua ka puta i roto i te picker.
- **Ia wāhi:** tuwhera **Tautuhinga → Kaitirotiro tuhinga** o tētahi wāhi. Ka āhei ia mara ki te tōpū
  i te taunoa ā-ao rānei ki te huria ki runga rānei iho mō taua wāhi anake.

Ka huna te hurihanga o tētahi mara i roto i te kaitirotiro — tae atu ki tōna tepe me ngā tōrangitia ētita —
ahakoa kua tautuhia e koe tētahi uara i mua. Kāore te uara e mukua: huria anō te mara ki runga, ā, ka puta anō.
