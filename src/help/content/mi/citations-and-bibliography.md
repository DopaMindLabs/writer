# Tūtohutanga me te rārangi pukapuka

He waihanga tūtohutanga ōkawa mō ia wāhi hei whakahaere tūāhua, me te **BibTeX**
kawemai me te kaweatu katoa.

## I roto i te tirohanga

- **Tāpirihia** tētahi tohutoro ā-ringa, rānei **kawemai** tētahi kōnae `.bib` / BibTeX kua tāhurihia.
- **Rapua**, **ētitahia**, me te **mukua** ngā urunga; ka prōhiia ngā taurua i te wā kawemai.
- **Kōwhiria-nui** hei tautuhia momo rānei hei muku i ngā mea maha i te wā kotahi.
- **Kaweatungia** te whare pukapuka katoa ki `.bib`.

## Te kawemai i ngā tohutoro

Tāhurihia rānei kawemaia BibTeX, ā, ka tāpiritia ngā urunga ki te whare pukapuka o te wāhi. Ka
pānuitia e te kaitatau ngā papa tōkena BibTeX me **ngā momo urunga rereke**, ā, ka kōrerotia
e hia i tāpiritia ki ērā i prōhiia hei **taurua**.

## Te whakahaere i te whare pukapuka

- Tiakihia ā **rapua** ngā urunga i roto i te papa tūtohutanga; ka **whakatea** tētahi pātene
  i te rārangi katoa.
- Tuwhera tētahi urunga mō tētahi **tirohanga taipitopito**, hurihia **ētita**, ā, tiakina i roto tonu.
- **Kōwhiria-nui** ia raina rānei kōwhiria-katoa hei tautuhia momo rānei hei muku mā tētahi
  kōrero whakaae.

## Te kaweatu

Kaweatungia te whare pukapuka ki BibTeX i noa atu hei whakamahi i wāhi kē rānei hei pupuri
tētahi tūāhuatanga tata ki ōu kuputuhi. Tirohia [Ō Raraunga](your-data) mō ngā kōwhiringa kaweatu katoa.

## Papapātuhi

I roto i te papa tūtohutanga:

- **Esc** — kati i te papa (rānei tāmatea tētahi ētita e haere ana).
- **Ctrl/⌘ + Enter** — tiakina te urunga e ētitahia ana e koe.

## Pānga

- [BrainSpace](brainspace) — honoa ngā puna ki ngā whakaaro e tautokotia ana e rātou.
- [Ō Raraunga](your-data) — kaweatu me te tiaki tūāhuatanga o tōu whare pukapuka.
