# Tīmata

He wāhi ōkawa-tuatahi hei tuhituhi. Ka noho tōu mahi i roto i ngā **wāhi** ā, ka tiakina ki
tōu pūtirotiro aunoa. Kāore ōkawa, kāore i te kapua, kāore he pātene tiaki.

## I roto i te tirohanga

1. Mai i **Kāinga**, tuwhera rānei hangaia tētahi wāhi.
2. Tāpirihia tētahi tuhinga mai i te **pātaka**.
3. Tīmata ki te pātuhi — ka tiakina ia pātuhi.

Ko tērā te hurihanga katoa. Ko ngā mea katoa i raro ko ngā taipitopito ka āhei koe ki te hoki mai.

## Tōu tuhinga tuatahi

- Ko tētahi tuhinga he wāhi anake hei tuhituhi — kāore he kōrero "kōnae hou".
- Whakaingoa anō i noa atu mā te pāwhiri i tōna ingoa i roto i te **paepae o runga**.
- Kāore i whakaingoatia? Ka whakatauhia tētahi taitara mai i tōu raina tuatahi.

## Te rapu i tōu huarahi

- **Pātaka** — ōu wāhi, ngā wāhanga, me ngā tuhinga.
- **Paepae o runga** — te tuhinga o nāianei me ngā tānga aratahi.
- Hononga **Āwhina** i roto i te whakatere whārangi — ka hoki mai ki konei i noa atu.

## Ngā haerenga ārahi

I te tuatahi o tōu tuwhera i tētahi mata matua, ka tohu tētahi haerenga ārahi poto i ngā
mea nui. Tīmata tētahi haerenga mā-waenga ā, ka mutu ngā haerenga ki te tīmata aunoa
i ērā atu mata hoki. Tākaro anō tētahi haerenga i noa atu i noa atu mai i te tahua āwhina (**?**)
rānei [Tautuhinga Tere](customization-and-settings#quick-settings).
