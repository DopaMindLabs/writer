# Whakaraupapa i tōu mahi

Ko tētahi raupapa māmā e tiaki ana i ngā kaupeka: ko ngā **wāhi** e pupuri ana i ngā **wāhanga**,
ā, ko ngā wāhanga e pupuri ana i ngā **tuhinga**.

## I roto i te tirohanga

- **Wāhi** — kotahi kaupeka-ōkawa (he pukapuka, he akoranga, he kaupeka).
- **Wāhanga** — tētahi rōpū tuhinga e hāngai ana i roto i tētahi wāhi.
- **Tuhinga** — te wāhi e tuhituhi ana koe.
- Tīmata tētahi wāhi hou mai i tētahi **tauira** hei mati i te whārangi kūare.

## Ngā wāhi

Ko tētahi wāhi he kaupeka-ōkawa me tōna ake pātaka, tautuhinga, me tōna ake ataahua
[BrainSpace](brainspace). Huria ngā wāhi mai i te pātaka rānei i te mata **Kāinga**.

## Ngā wāhanga me ngā tuhinga

I roto i tētahi wāhi, rōpūhia ngā tuhinga e hāngai ana ki roto i ngā wāhanga. **Tāpiria**, **whakaingoa anō**, ā,
raupapahia anō ngā tuhinga mai i te pātaka. Pātuhi **Ātete** hei tāmatea tētahi tāpiri rānei whakaingoa anō
me te kore tiaki.

## Ngā tauira

I tōu hanga i tētahi mea hou, tīmata mai i tētahi **tauira** kaua i tētahi whārangi kūare.
Ka whakakī ngā tauira i tētahi hanganga — ngā wāhanga me ngā tuhinga tīmatanga — nō reira ka
tīmata koe me te kaha.

## Te whakaingoa anō me te whakatika

- Pāwhiria tēhea ingoa i roto i te pātaka hei whakaingoa anō; ko **Enter** e mau ana, ko **Ātete** e huri.
- Pupuritia ngā tuhinga poto me te arotahi; whakahāngaitia ngā wāhanga mō te hanganga.
- Whakamahia ngā taitara mārama kia noho āta tirohia ai te rapua me te pātaka.
