# Ngaahi Me'angāue Kotoa

Ko e me'a kotoa 'e lava 'o fai 'e he ngāue, 'i ha lisi 'e lava ke sio vave. Ko e me'a
kotoa 'oku hōhoko ki hono teuteu ke ke teke hangatonu ki he fakaikiiki.

## Tohi & Liliu

- **Tauhi-Otometi** — ko e kī-boto kotoa 'oku tauhi fakalokolo, 'ikai ha faka'eli tauhi. [Ako lahi ange](writing-and-editing#autosave)
- **Tooli-pa Fofolo** — ngaahi fōtunga vave 'i hono fili 'o e tohi. [Ako lahi ange](writing-and-editing#the-floating-toolbar)
- **Fakahingoa Tohi** — lave ki he hingoa 'i he pā 'olu'olu. [Ako lahi ange](writing-and-editing#renaming-a-document)
- **Nofo** — ko ho'o talateu 'oku mo'ui 'i he to'o-ki-ai lahi. [Ako lahi ange](your-data#what-local-first-means)

## Ngaahi Fōtunga

- **Markdown Fakapoto** — ngaahi 'ulu, ngaahi lisi, ngaahi kuomu, fakalahi/fakasalole 'i ho'o tohi. [Ako lahi ange](formatting-and-markdown#markdown-shortcuts)
- **Ngaahi 'Ulu 'oku langa 'a e outline** — faka'ilonga 'a e ngaahi me'a lōloa. [Ako lahi ange](formatting-and-markdown#headings-and-structure)

## Fakakatoa
