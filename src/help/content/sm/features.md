# Fa'asologa atoa o mea e mafai

O mea uma e mafai e le polokalame, i se lisi e mafai ona su'ega. E feso'ota'i
fa'asologa ta'itasi i lana fa'atonuga ina ia mafai ona e soso'o sa'o i le
fa'amatalaga.

## Tusitusiga & fa'atulaga

- **Fa'asao otometi** — ki ta'itasi e fa'asao sa'o, e leai se fa'ailoilo fa'asao. [A'oa'o atili](writing-and-editing#autosave)
- **Faaaupega fa'alele** — fa'atulagaga vave pe filifilia le tusitusiga. [A'oa'o atili](writing-and-editing#the-floating-toolbar)
- **Fa'aigoaina pepa** — kiliki le ulutala i le faaupega i luga. [A'oa'o atili](writing-and-editing#renaming-a-document)
- **Tumau** — o lou tali e tumau e tali atu i le fa'afouina malosi. [A'oa'o atili](your-data#what-local-first-means)

## Fa'atulagaga

- **Fa'ailoga fa'apuupuu markdown** — ulutala, lisi, tauloto, 'aloa'alo/italic pe a e taina. [A'oa'o atili](formatting-and-markdown#markdown-shortcuts)
- **O ulutala e fausia le fa'asologa** — fa'atulagaina vaega umi. [A'oa'o atili](formatting-and-markdown#headings-and-structure)

## Fa'atulagaina galuega

- **Spaces** — poloketi e nofoa'i. [A'oa'o atili](organizing-your-work#spaces)
- **Itu & pepa** — fa'aopoopo, fa'aigoaina, ma toe fa'atulagaina mai le saolaga. [A'oa'o atili](organizing-your-work#sections-and-documents)
- **Fa'ata'ita'i** — amata mai se fausaga, ae le o se itulau gaogao. [A'oa'o atili](organizing-your-work#templates)

## Va'aiga & auivi

- **Tusi** — le fa'aogaina fa'atonu fa'aletonu. [A'oa'o atili](views-and-modes#write)

- **Va'alua** — pepa e lua i tafatafa. [A'oa'o atili](views-and-modes#split)
- **Iloiloga** — fa'asologa, fa'amatalaga, ma tala'aga. [A'oa'o atili](views-and-modes#the-inspector)
