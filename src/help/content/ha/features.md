# Dukkan fasalolin

Duk abin da app din zai iya yi, a cikin jerin da za a iya dubawa cikin sauri. Kowane abu yana haɗe zuwa jagora nai don ku iya tsalle kai tsaye zuwa cikakken bayani.

## Rubutu da gyara

- **Autosave** — kowane maɓalli an adana shi a gida, babu maɓallin ajiye. [Ƙara koyo](writing-and-editing#autosave)
- **Kayan aikin da ke iyo** — tsarin sauri akan zaɓen rubutu. [Ƙara koyo](writing-and-editing#the-floating-toolbar)
- **Sake suna takaddun** — danna suna a sanduna na sama. [Ƙara koyo](writing-and-editing#renaming-a-document)
- **Dawwama** — daftarin ku yana tsira daga sake lodi mai ƙarfi. [Ƙara koyo](your-data#what-local-first-means)

## Tsari

- **Taken suna gina shimfidar** — tsara dogon aiki. [Ƙara koyo](formatting-and-markdown#headings-and-structure)

## Tsarawa
