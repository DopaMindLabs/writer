# Tsari da markdown

Tsara rubutu ta hanyoyi uku: kayan aikin da ke iyo, gajerun hanyoyin madanni, ko gajerun hanyoyin markdown da aka buga yayin da kuke rubutu.

## Takaitaccen bayani

- Buga `## ` don taken, `- ` don guntu, `1. ` don jerin lamba.
- Kewaya kalma da `**mai ƙarfi**` ko `*kwatsam*`.
- Zaɓi rubutu don amfani da kayan aikin da ke iyo maimakon haka.

## Gajerun hanyoyin markdown

Buga waɗannan a farkon layi (ko a kusa da kalma) kuma suna canzawa kai tsaye:

- `# ` → taken 1, `## ` → taken 2, `### ` → taken 3
- `- ` ko `* ` → jerin guntu
- `1. ` → jerin lamba
- `> ` → ambaton toshe
- `**mai ƙarfi**` → **mai ƙarfi**, `*kwatsam*` → *kwatsam*

## Tsari cikin layi

Zaɓi rubutu kuma yi amfani da kayan aikin da ke iyo, ko yi amfani da madannai:

- **Mai ƙarfi** — gajeriyar hanyar mai ƙarfi
- *Kwatsam* — gajeriyar hanyar kwatsam

## Taken da tsari

Taken suna bayyana **shimfida** ta takarda da ake nuna a cikin mai dubawa. Yi amfani da su don kiyaye dogon aiki mai saukin tafiya.

## Lissafi da ambatoni

Lissafi da ambatoni na toshe suna kiyaye tsarinsu lokacin da kuka latsa Enter, don haka za ku iya ci gaba da rubutu ba tare da kaiwa hannun mouse ba. Latsa Enter a kan ƙwallon jerin fanko don fita daga jerin.
