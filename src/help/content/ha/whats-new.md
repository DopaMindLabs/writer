# Abubuwan da suka saba

Sabbin ingantaccen abubuwa na LIpsum Writer, sabon farko. Don cikakken tarihin canje-canje, duba bayanan sakin aikin a GitHub.

## Yuni 2026

- **Ra'ayin rabo a wayoyin hannu** — yanzu faranti biyu suna tara sama da ƙasa a tsaye kuma suna zaune gefe da gefe a kwance. Duba [Wayar hannu](mobile).
- **Mai dubawa takarda a wayar hannu** — bude shimfida, bayani, tarihi, da ayyuka daga shafin **ƙari**. Duba [Mai dubawa takarda](doc-inspector).
- **Yawon shakatawa suna girmama tsallewa** — kori yawon shakatawa a tsakiya kuma yawon shakatawa sun daina fara kai tsaye a allon sauran. Sake kunna kowane yawon shakatawa daga menu na taimako (**?**).
- **Matsayin haɗa-fayil a ganinta** — allon gida yana nuna guntun gargaɗi har sai an kunna haɗa-fayil da ajiyewa. Duba [Bayananku](your-data).
- **Tuntuɓi mu** — tuntuɓe mu daga menu na **ƙari**; yana buɗe sabon matsala a tracker na aikin.
