# Ambatoni da jerin kwalekwale

Kowace wuri tana da ɗakin karatu na ambatoni nsu don sarrafa manazarta, tare da cikakken shigo da fitar da **BibTeX**.

## Takaitaccen bayani

- **Ƙara** manazarta da hannu, ko **shigo da** fayil `.bib` / manna BibTeX.
- **Bincika**, **gyara**, da **share** shigarwar; ana tsallake kwafi yayin shigo da bayani.
- **Zaɓi da yawa** don saita nau'i ko share da yawa a lokaci guda.
- **Fitar da** dukan ɗakin karatu zuwa `.bib`.

## Shigo da manazarta

Manna ko shigo da BibTeX kuma ana ƙara shigarwar zuwa ɗakin karatu na wuri. Mai fassara yana karanta filayen BibTeX na ma'auni da **nau'in shigarwar daban-daban**, kuma yana bayar da rahoto kan nawa aka ƙara dangane da aka tsallake a matsayin **kwafi**.

## Sarrafa ɗakin karatu

- Duba da **bincika** shigarwar a cikin rukunin ambatoni; maɓallin **share** yana maido da cikakken jeri.
- Buɗe shigarwa don **ra'ayin cikakkun bayanai**, canzawa **gyara**, kuma ajiye cikin layi.
- **Zaɓi da yawa** a kowane jeri ko zaɓi-duka don saita nau'i ko share ta hanyar tattaunawa ta tabbatarwa.

## Fitar da bayani

Fitar da ɗakin karatu zuwa BibTeX a kowane lokaci don amfani da shi a wani wuri ko don kiyaye ajiye waje kusa da adabinku. Duba [Bayananku](your-data) don cikakkun zaɓuɓɓukan fitar da bayani.

## Madannai

A cikin rukunin ambatoni:

- **Esc** — rufe rukunin (ko soke gyara da ke ci gaba).
- **Ctrl/⌘ + Enter** — ajiye shigarwar da kuke gyarawa.

## Masu alaƙa

- [BrainSpace](brainspace) — haɗa tushe zuwa ra'ayoyin da suke goyan baya.
- [Bayananku](your-data) — fitar da bayani kuma ajiye ɗakin karatunku.
