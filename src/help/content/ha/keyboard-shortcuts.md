# Gajerun hanyoyin madanni

Kiyaye hannuwanku a madannai. A macOS yi amfani da **⌘**; a Windows da Linux yi amfani da **Ctrl**.

## Takaitaccen bayani

- **⌘K** — buɗe Taimakon Sauri daga ko'ina.
- **⌘\\** — canza yanayin mayar da hankali.
- **Esc** — rufe ɓoyayyen abu ko soke gyara.
- **Ctrl/⌘ + Enter** — ajiye ambaton da kuke gyarawa.

## Na duniya

- **⌘K** — buɗe ɓoyayyen **Taimakon Sauri** daga ko'ina. Bincika Help Center ko tsalle zuwa gajeriyar hanya, sannan latsa **Esc** don rufe.
- **⌘\\** — canza [yanayin mayar da hankali](views-and-modes#focus) kashe da kunna.

## Markdown na mai gyara

Ana buga a farkon layi, waɗannan suna canzawa yayin da kuke rubutu:

- `# ` / `## ` / `### ` — taken
- `- ` / `* ` — jerin guntu
- `1. ` — jerin lamba
- `> ` — ambaton toshe

Kewaya kalma da `**` don mai ƙarfi ko `*` don kwatsam.

## Rukunin ambatoni

- **Esc** — rufe rukunin (ko soke gyara da ke ci gaba)
- **Ctrl/⌘ + Enter** — ajiye shigarwar da kuke gyarawa

## Bayani kan gajerun hanyoyin mai bincike

'Yan haɗin maɓalli an ajiye su ta mai binciken ku ko tsarin aiki. Inda app din ya bayyana gajeriyar hanya, yana samun fifiko a allon nsa; a wani wuri tsarin da zaɓin ya lashe.
