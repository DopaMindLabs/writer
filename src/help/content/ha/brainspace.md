# BrainSpace

Zane don ɗaukar bayanai marasa tsari, ra'ayoyi, da guntun rubutu kafin su zama adabi. Kowace wuri tana da ɗaya.

## Takaitaccen bayani

- **Ƙara bayana** daga kayan aiki; buɗe shi a **jawo-gefe na cikakkun bayanai** don gyara.
- Gyara **take** ko **jikin** bayana cikin layi, kai tsaye a kan zane.
- Ba bayanai **nau'i** don rarrabance ra'ayoyi, tambayoyi, da tushe.
- Zana **haɗin gwiwa** tsakanin bayanai, kuma **haɗa bayana zuwa takarda**.
- Makala har zuwa **hotuna biyu** kowace bayana.

## Bayanai

Ƙara bayanai cikin 'yanci a kan zane. Bayana na iya ɗauke da tunani mai sauri, jimla, ko aiki. Sabbin bayanai suna bayyana a **ra'ayinka na yanzu** — ko da kun yi guguwa daga inda kuka fara — don haka katin da kuka ƙara yana koyaushe a inda kuke kallo. Danna take ko jikin bayana don **gyara cikin layi**, ko buɗe **jawo-gefe na cikakkun bayanai** don cikakken ra'ayi. Danna dama a kan bayana don menu na mahallinsa; **Escape** yana korar shi. Gefe yana nuna **ƙidayan bayanai marasa tsari**.

## Nau'in bayanai

Bayanai suna zuwa da **nau'i** daban-daban don ku iya rarrabance ra'ayi daga tambaya daga tushe a ganinta — tsari mai sauƙi a kan zane wanda ba shi da tsari.

## Haɗin gwiwa

Zana **haɗin gwiwa** tsakanin bayanai don tsara yadda ra'ayoyi ke da alaƙa.

## Hotuna

Makala hotuna zuwa bayana daga jawo-gefe na cikakkun bayanai (har zuwa **biyu kowace bayana**). Ana ajiye su a gida a duk sake lodi kuma ana iya cire su a kowane lokaci.

### Kalli hoto cikin cikakken girma

Danna kowane hoto — thumbnail a kan bayana, a cikin jawo-gefe na cikakkun bayanai, ko hoton mai girma a kan katin hoto — don buɗe shi cikin cikakken girma. Idan bayana tana ɗauke da fiye da ɗaya hoto, shafa tsakaninsu da kibiyoyin da ke kan allon ko kibiyoyin Hagu da Dama na madannin ku. Latsa **Escape** ko danna wajen hoto don rufe shi.

### Katunan hoto

Don bayana da hoto a gaba, ƙara katin **hoto** daga kayan aiki na zane. Katin hoto yana nuna hoto mai girma a maimakon rubutun jikin, tare da take a matsayin alama zaɓi. Sabon katin hoto yana farawa da yankin jefa **ƙara hoto** — danna shi don zaɓar hotunka. Katunan hoto suna ɗauke da har zuwa hotuna biyu kuma suna haɗewa da sauran bayanai kamar kowane kati.
