# Ƙalamai da faɗaɗawa

Alama rubutunka don nuna sassa, bar bayanai ga kanku, da bibiyar abin da ke buƙatar aiki.

## Takaitaccen bayani

- **Zaɓi rubutu** kuma yi amfani da **faɗaɗawa** daga palette na launi.
- Yi amfani da launuka a matsayin lambar — misali rawaya "sake duba", pink "layi mai ƙarfi".
- Faɗaɗawa suna daidaitawa da jigokin ku, suna kasancewa masu karatu ko'ina.

## Faɗaɗawa

Zaɓi rubutu kuma yi amfani da faɗaɗawa. Faɗaɗawa suna amfani da ƙaramin **palette** na launuka don ku iya gina ma'anarku.

## Zaɓar palette

Palette na faɗaɗawa yana daidaita a cikin rubutunka kuma yana **girmama jigo na yanzu**, don haka faɗaɗawa suna kasancewa masu karatu a haske, duhu, da yanayin bambancin launi mai ƙarfi iri ɗaya. Daidaita jigo da bayyanawa a [Keɓancewa da saitunan](customization-and-settings).

## Ƙalamai

Makala bayana zuwa sashi lokacin da faɗaɗawa kaɗai bai isa ba. Ƙalamai suna kiyaye tunannin gyara ku kusa da rubutun da yake nufin shi, ba tare da takura adabin da kansa ba.

## Nasihu

- Yanke shawarar abin da kowane launi ke nufi kafin ku fara, kuma ku kasance daidai.
- Yi amfani da faɗaɗawa a matsayin jerin duba na bita — share su yayin da kuke warware kowane ɗaya.
- Canza bayana zuwa cikakkiyar ra'ayi a [BrainSpace](brainspace).

## Masu alaƙa

- [BrainSpace](brainspace) — ƙara haɓaka ra'ayin da aka alama.
- [Keɓancewa da saitunan](customization-and-settings) — jigogi da bayyanawa.
