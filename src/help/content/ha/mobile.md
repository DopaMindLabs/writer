# Wayar hannu

App din yana daidaitawa ga allunan ƙanana don ku iya karatu da rubutu a wayar hannu ko allunan.

## Takaitaccen bayani

- **Jerin alamun ƙasa** yana maye gurbin gefe a allunan ƙanana.
- Tab ɗin **ƙari** yana buɗe jawo-gefe tare da saitunan, taimako, da game da.
- Ana iya amfani da yanayin **Karatu** da [BrainSpace](brainspace) a wayar hannu.
- **Ra'ayin rabo** yana samuwa a allunan mafi girma.

## Tafiya a allunan ƙanana

A allunan masu kunkuntar, gefe yana lalacewa a bayan menu kuma tafiya ta **ƙananan-tabs** ta bayyana. Taɓa tab don canza yanayi, bude **jawo-gefe na tafiya** don kai wurare da takaddun, kuma bude **ƙari** don saitunan, **Taimako**, da game da.

## Abin da ke aiki da kyau a wayar hannu

- Karatu da nazari daftarori a yanayin **karatu**.
- Gyare-gyare kaɗan da ɗaukar ra'ayoyi a [BrainSpace](brainspace).
- Tsari mai sauƙi ta hanyar kayan aikin da ke iyo.

## Sake suna takarda

A cikin jawo-gefe na tafiya, taɓa maɓallin **⋮** a jerin takarda kuma zaɓi **Sake suna** don ba takarda suna sabon.

## Mai dubawa takarda

Bude tab ɗin **ƙari** kuma zaɓi **Mai dubawa takarda** don shigar da [mai dubawa takarda](doc-inspector) — shimfida, bayani, tarihi, da ayyuka — daga dama.

## Abin da yake mafi kyau a manyan allunan

- Aikin tsari mai nauyi a cikin takaddun da yawa.
- [Ra'ayin rabo](views-and-modes#split) — kwatanta takaddun biyu gefe da gefe.

## Nasihu

- Ajiyewa yana gida ga kowane mai bincike, don haka aikin da aka fara a wayar hannunku yana kan wayar hannunku sai kun fitar da shi kuma ku shigo da shi — duba [Bayananku](your-data).
- Juyawa zuwa kwance don ma'aunin rubutu mai kwanciyar hankali.

## Masu alaƙa

- [Ra'ayoyi da yanayi](views-and-modes) — abin da kowane yanayi yake yi.
- [Bayananku](your-data) — motsa aiki tsakanin na'urori.
