# Keɓancewa da saitunan

Daidaita jigo, rubutun allo, da faɗin karatu don dacewa da ku. Bude allon **Saitunan duniya** daga tafiyar shafi, ko canza mahimman abubuwa cikin sauri daga **Saitunan sauri**.

## Takaitaccen bayani

- **Jigo** — haske, duhu, ko bambancin launi mai ƙarfi.
- **Rubutun allo** — rubutun adabi da girma.
- **Faɗin karatu** — faɗin shafi na rubutu.
- **Zaɓuɓɓukan mai gyara** — misali kayan aikin da ke iyo.
- Maƙullai mafi amfani suna a [Saitunan sauri](customization-and-settings#quick-settings).

## Saitunan sauri

Popover na **Saitunan sauri** yana ba ku maƙullai na yau da kullun ba tare da barin takardanku ba. Buɗe shi daga hanyar app. Ana tara maƙullai bisa manufa:

- **Bayyanawa** — canzawa **jigo** da **faɗin karatu**.
- **Rubutu** — canzawa **yanayin mayar da hankali** da **kayan aikin da ke iyo**.
- **Yawon taimako** — sake kunna yawon shakatawa na jagora.
- **Ƙari** — abubuwa sabon, saukin shiga, da game da.
- Haɗin **Help Center →** (irin wannan yadda yake haɗewa zuwa **saitunan duniya →**), don taimako da saitunan suna koyaushe danna ɗaya nesa.

## Nemo saitunan

Allon Saitunan duniya yana tara zaɓuɓɓuka cikin **Abubuwan da ake so**, **Rubutu**, **Bayani**, da **Asusun**. Kowace ƙungiya shafi ɗaya ne tare da sasannin ta da aka tara sama zuwa ƙasa. Zaɓi abin a cikin tafiyar gefen kuma shafi zai tafi kai tsaye zuwa wannan sashi — kuma tafiyar yana nuna haske a sashi da kuke karatu yayin da kuke gusawa.

## Jigogi

Zaɓi **haske**, **duhu**, ko jigo na **bambancin launi mai ƙarfi**. An tsara bambancin launi mai ƙarfi don saukin karatu da saukin shiga, kuma kowane ɓangare na app — ciki har da faɗaɗawa da Help Center — yana daidaitawa kai tsaye. Duba [Saukin Shiga](accessibility) don cikakken saffar abubuwan da ake so.

## Rubutun allo

Daidaita rubutun adabi da girma don dacewa da idanunku.

## Faɗin karatu

Saita faɗin shafi na rubutu. Ma'aunin mafi kunkuntar na iya sauƙaƙa karanta adabi mai tauri; faɗi ya ɗauke da ƙari a allon.

## Zaɓuɓɓukan mai gyara

Canza ɗabi'ar mai gyara kamar **kayan aikin da ke iyo** don dacewa da yadda kuke son aiki.

## Masu alaƙa

- [Saukin Shiga](accessibility) — bambancin launi, motsi, girman rubutu, da mayar da hankali.
- [Gajerun hanyoyin madanni](keyboard-shortcuts) — tafiya komai daga madanni.
- [Bayananku](your-data) — fitar da bayani, shigo da bayani, da ajiye aikinku.
